==============================================================================
Acrylic DNS Proxy Console
==============================================================================

Usage:
  AcrylicConsole.exe [/NoBanner] [/NoLog]

Options:
  /NoBanner
    Does not write the application banner to the console on startup.
  /NoLog
    Does not write the application log to the console while running.

Examples:
    AcrylicConsole.exe
    AcrylicConsole.exe /NoBanner /NoLog
